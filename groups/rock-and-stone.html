About Us

Rock and Stone is an inclusive outdoor and nature group that welcomes everyone who wants to explore and connect with the natural world. We organize various outdoor activities that cater to all skill levels and abilities.

Our group creates accessible opportunities for hiking, nature walks, rock climbing, and outdoor education. We believe that nature belongs to everyone, and we work to break down barriers that traditionally exclude marginalized communities from outdoor spaces.
