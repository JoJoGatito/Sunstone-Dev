About Us

Our Hue House (Black, Indigenous, and People of Color) group creates a dedicated space for community members of color to connect, share experiences, and celebrate our diverse cultures and identities. We focus on building solidarity, fostering understanding, and creating opportunities for authentic connection.

Through our gatherings and events, we work to address the unique challenges faced by BIPOC in Southern Colorado while celebrating our strengths, traditions, and contributions to the community. We create spaces where members can be their authentic selves and find support among others who share similar experiences.
